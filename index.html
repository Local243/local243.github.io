About Local 243

Based in the Niagara region, OPSEU SEFPO Local 243 represents full-time and part-time support staff at Niagara College. We are dedicated to protecting our members' rights, ensuring fair and safe working conditions, and strengthening the overall campus community for both staff and students.

As part of OPSEU SEFPO's College Support division, our collective efforts ensure that the essential work performed by support staff is recognized and respected. We are committed to achieving a better, more secure future for all public college workers.
